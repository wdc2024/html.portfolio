About Me

Welcome to my creative world!

I’m Denis Wasike, a passionate photographer and videographer based in Nanyuki, Kenya. With over 5 years of experience capturing the beauty of life through my lens, I specialize in lifestyle photography, wedding photography, portrait photography, and commercial videography.

My Journey

My love for photography and videography began at a young age, inspired by street and studio photographers including movies. Over the years, I’ve honed my craft through other photographers i.e Favier Productions, Njeri Photographt, Tony Trivet, Canon workshops and Online Courses and Tutorials. Today, I’m proud to offer a unique blend of technical skill and artistic vision in every project I undertake.
